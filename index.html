Twelve Drawings
1930s definitions of Twelve Steps words - plus recent illustrations

Steps

When possible, the steps were worked with the help of a sover sponsor. Still, in the early days, many people recovered by following the written literature alone.


Promises

In the description of Step Nine, there is a long paragraph listing benefits that can be expected in recovery. These have come affectionatley to be known as the Twelve Promises.


Serenity Words

Years after the steps were written, another set of principles were published. Known as the "Traditions", they guide Twelve Steps groups toward cohesiveness.


Moments

In the literature, several stories were used to sum up...

Nicotine, valium, vicodin, marijuana, ecstasy and alcohol.
